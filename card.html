Snow Leopard

The snow leopard cannot roar, despite possessing partial ossification of the hyoid bone.

  • Scientific Name: Uncia uncia
  • Average Length: 175-250 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 18 years
  • Habitat: High Mountain Plateau

The snow leopard is a large cat native to the mountain ranges of Central and South Asia.
